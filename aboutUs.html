    AfroTada

    On June 20, 2020, Zainab Olaitan and Simotwo Zainabu birthed AfroTada, at the beginning all they had was a beautiful idea and their joint capabilities.

    AfroTada is an initiative that aims to curate and preserve African content. We recognize that the proper curation and preservation of African content (books, people, traditions, languages, Universities, Historical Sites etc.) will stimulate the awakening of the African consciousness and bring us closer to the knowledge of our origin as a continent.

    AfroTada was created due to the need for a platform by Africans where authentic stories and information about Africa are shared. We are here to collate and provide you with those facts, figures and stories about Africa that you want to know but have not found a source for.

    The name AfroTada literally means Africa Awakening. (Afro derived from Africa and Tada from the Hausa word for stimulate which can mean awakening.

    Our Vision

    To be the fountain of African knowledge

    Our Mission

    To ensure the proper curation and preservation of African content

    Our Objectives

  • To preserve African content
  • To curate content that tells the African story
  • To be a one stop place for African content
  • To awaken the consciousness of Africans through knowledge of their origin
