    Young Professional Africa

    Jonathan Ruwanika and Shaneil Moodley seek to tackle issues facing young professionals in an African context via shared experiences and storytelling. They also use their collective African and professional networks as a pipeline for guests on the show.

    African Tech RoundUp

    The African Tech Roundup podcast delivers independent Africa-focused technology, digital and innovation insight and analysis. The show is produced by broadcaster and entrepreneur, Andile Masuku and co-hosted by founder and writer of The Subtext Osarumen Osamuyi.

    Alibi

    Alibi is an award-winning, South African true crime podcast. The first series is about a man who may have been wrongfully convicted for 17 years. Anthony De Vries was convicted for double murder and robbery under apartheid. But he might be innocent. When we first meet Anthony, he has already served 17 years for his crimes. We investigate if the claim of his innocence is right. Has a man been locked-up wrongfully for most of his life? And is it somehow linked to the cops who put him inside?

    Sound Africa

    Sound Africa is a homegrown podcast organisation passionate about producing original audio content that upends the weary clichés we’ve become accustomed to in the coverage of Africa. We seek out the untold and obscure, go beyond surface-level coverage of big events, and shed light on matters of public interest without losing sight of the human interest. In doing so we aim to amplify African voices through the power of digital audio and the internet.
